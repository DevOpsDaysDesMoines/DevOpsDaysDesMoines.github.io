About DevOpsDays

DevOpsDays is a worldwide community conference series for anyone interested in IT improvement.

Be a part of the first ever DevOpsDays in Iowa!

DevOpsDays is a worldwide series of technical conferences that started in Ghent, Belgium in 2009. In 2017, there were over 50 DevOpsDays events held across the globe.

On April 12th and 13th, Des Moines will be hosting its first DevOpsDay at the Community Choice Credit Union Convention Center!

Topics will include software development, IT infrastructure operation, automation, testing, security and organizational cultures.

DevOpsDays is dedicated to providing an environment where everyone in the devops community can learn and share in respectful, considerate collaboration.

In that effort we have a code of conduct that we expect everyone involved with the conference to follow.
